Facility Of MSPVL

Hostel

  • Accommodation for over 400 students.
  • All rooms are provided with Fans and Tables.
  • Modern Kitchen to provide Hygienic Food at reasonable rate.
  • Adequate number of Bathrooms / Toilets.
  • Phone facility for communication.
  • Adequate recreation facilities.
  • 63 KVA Generator.

Library

Our Library plays a vital role in the curriculum development of students and is well equipped with more than 30,000 volumes of technical books and 50 National, International, Technical Journals & Magazines.

  • Journals
  • Technical Magazines
  • Daily Newspapers
  • Multimedia Packages containing 3039 CDs, 35 Audios and 16 Videos

Physical Education, Indoor Stadium

Our college also gives more importance to the sports activities of the students. Our students have participated in many games and sports in District and State Level and won many prizes. Facilities for playing Football, Hockey, Cricket, Volleyball, Badminton, Table Tennis, Chess, and Athletics etc are available for the students. An excellent Badminton Court and fully equipped Gym are also available for the students.

Transport

Twenty one buses are run from our college to Tenkasi, Shenkottai, V.K.Puram, Ambasamudram, Kadayam Surandai and Alangulam for the benefit of students at reasonable cost. We have two vans for our college and Hostel usage.

Canteen

Our canteen having 336 seating capacity is provided for Day Scholar Students. Hygienic food and snacks are supplied to students and staff at reasonable cost.

Dispensary

Timely Medical assistance is made possible through the dispensary at the college. The dispensary is well equipped with a Staff Nurse and also provide First Aid facilities to both students and staff.

Mineral water

In our college,the drinking water has been purified by modern treatment technology using Reverse osmosis.The R.O. plant is in operation to fulfill the drinking water needs of all the students, staff members, supporting staff and the visitors. The maximum capacity of plant is 1,000 liters per hour. The RO treated drinking water is dispensed through 10 Water Coolers in the college provided at various locations that include Class Room Blocks, Laboratory Blocks, Administrative Block and other Centres .
